Git Cooking!

Get ideas for food and drink pairings with our food-drink generator.

Everyone has to eat. And drinks make any meal more fun. You might have go-tos for Sunday brunch at home, date night and dinner parties—or maybe all you eat is instant ramen—but variety is the spice of life.

That’s why we created Git Cooking, a food and idea generator where anyone can discover new ways to expand their palette and experiment with new flavors (for better or for worse).
